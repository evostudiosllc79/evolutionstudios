Our Approach

A landscape isn't decoration — it's how your home meets the world.

We're a small Tacoma-based team building outdoor spaces the way they used to be built: with patience, good materials, and an eye toward what will still look beautiful in twenty years.

01

Native plants,
done right

We specialize in Pacific Northwest natives — salal, sword fern, Oregon grape, red-flowering currant. Lower maintenance, better for pollinators, and they thrive in our climate without heavy irrigation.

02

The same crew,
every visit

You won't see a rotating cast of subcontractors. Our four-person team shows up, knows your property, and treats every project like it's their own yard.

03

Built with
lasting materials

Natural stone, kiln-dried timber, quality concrete. We don't use the cheap stuff — because we're the ones who'd have to fix it when it fails.

What We Do

From design to installation — handled start to finish.

Every project starts with a free on-site consultation. We walk your property together, understand what you're hoping for, and map out a plan that fits your budget and timeline.

01 / Design

Landscape Design

Custom landscape plans built around your property's slope, light, soil, and vision. Renderings provided before any ground is broken.

 02 / Hardscape

Hardscaping & Stonework

Patios, walkways, retaining walls, and outdoor kitchens. Built with natural stone, pavers, or poured concrete — your choice of finish.

 03 / Gardens

Native Gardens

Plant palettes designed for the Pacific Northwest — beautiful year-round, lower water needs, and a haven for local pollinators.

 04 / Care

Seasonal Maintenance

Ongoing care from the same crew that built your landscape. Seasonal clean-ups, pruning, irrigation checks, and monthly visits.
