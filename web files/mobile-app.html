Design Essentials

What Makes a Great App

Mobile users are picky — they delete apps fast. Here's what I focus on to make sure yours sticks around.

Fast Onboarding

First impressions matter. I design onboarding flows that show value immediately without requiring a 20-step setup.

Thumb-Friendly Zones

Important actions are placed where thumbs can easily reach. Designed for one-handed use on large screens.

Minimal Clutter

Small screens demand focus. I strip away everything non-essential so users can act on what matters most.

Fast Performance

Designs are optimized for quick load times. Users abandon apps that take more than a few seconds to respond.

Smart Notifications

Push notifications that inform without annoying. Users stay engaged without feeling spammed.

Offline-First

Apps that work even without a connection. Users can keep using key features on planes, trains, or dead zones.
